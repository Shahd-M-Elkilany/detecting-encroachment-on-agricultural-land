Interpretation & Next Steps

🔍 Root cause of low AUC

34 positive examples across 75 tiles — with only 1 positive per CV fold, AUC is effectively binary (0, 0.5, or 1.0). The OOF AUC of 0.551 means the model scores the true positive above the negative in ~55% of tile pairs. Not strong, but non-trivial signal exists.

⚠️ Remaining overfit

Train AUC=1.0 persists even with max_depth=10. Next step: lower to max_depth=6 or 8, or add min_samples_split=5. Also consider that train/val/test splits may share similar spectral characteristics, inflating train metrics.

✅ Recommended next steps

1. Use threshold=0.19 for screening (100% test recall). 2. Try max_depth=6 to further reduce overfit. 3. Add SMOTE oversampling or synthetic pairs for underrepresented encroachment signatures. 4. Consider gradient boosting (XGBoost) for better calibration with imbalanced data.
